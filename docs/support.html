Hosted Service Authentication

All hosted service requests are authenticated via NIP-98 (Nostr HTTP auth) and paid via Bitcoin Lightning. Every request is cryptographically signed by the user's Nostr keypair, creating an attribution trail:

  • Your Nostr public key (npub) identifies you as the requester
  • Your NIP-98 signature proves you authorized the request
  • Your Lightning payment is tied to the specific request
